Acknowledgments

I am very grateful to the many people who made this book possible. First, I want to thank Carol McGeehan, who suggested that I write this book and who helped me in formulating its approach. I am grateful to Joe Terry and everyone at the Froebe Group for their assistance. As always, Wolters Kluwer has been everything an author could hope for and more.

Second, I am grateful for all the institutional support I received in completing the first edition of this book at the University of Southern California Law School and the second edition at Duke University School of Law. At USC, Deans Scott Bice and Matthew Spitzer were enormously supportive of this book and of my work. Darin Fox provided terrific computer assistance, and my assistant, Margaret Miller, was invaluable in helping me complete this book and supporting me in all my professional endeavors for 15 years. I prepared the third, fourth and fifth editions at the University of California, Irvine School of Law, where I was tremendously assisted by Brandy Stewart.
